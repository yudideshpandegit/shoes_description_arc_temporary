How To Measure?

Wear the type of socks you'll wear with your shoes and measure feet in the evening (feet typically swell during the day)

1

Take a piece of paper and place it on a firm floor, near a wall. Place your foot on the paper with your heel slightly touching the wall.

2

Mark with a pencil on, on the paper, the end of your longest toe for both feet.

3

Measure the length from the wall to the marking and take the longer of the measurements you took for each feet.

Compare with our size guide to fit the best size.
